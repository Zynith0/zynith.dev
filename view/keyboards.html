Keyboards

So, I am a complete keyboard nerd. This is pretty much just me talking about all the keyboards I own

One of the first mechanical keyboards I remember owning was the
HyperX Alloy Origins Core TKL with their blue switches

After that one, I believe it was the SteelSeries Apex Pro TKL

The Apex Pro TKL was unique, as it has Hall Effect switches
It was the first (and last lol) Hall Effect keyboard I've owned

Then I got the LeoBog Hi75

This was my first custom keyboard. I originally put LeoBog Greywood V3 switches in it
Eventually, I ended up putting Gateron Oil Kings in it

Then, I got my first ergo keyboard. The Corne

The corne is my 2nd favorite keyboard that I own
It's a really nice, small ergo keyboard

Originally, I had Gateron Oil Kings in it, but I switched them for Kailh Box Jades

After that, I got my favorite keyboard that I own

The Kinesis Advantage 360

The Kinesis Advantage 360 is genuinely an incredible keyboard
The concavity of it makes it so comfortable

I really like the general layout of the keyboard as well

So yeah, those are all the keyboards I own.
